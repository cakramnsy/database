Project Description:

Project Description:

As a 3D Generalist, I undertook the challenge of recreating a highly detailed and realistic 3D model of the iconic AQUA Water gallon, a widely recognized product from Indonesia. This project was conceived as part of my personal portfolio to showcase my modeling and texturing skills, with the goal of selling the asset online on 3D marketplace platforms such as Sketchfab. The 3D model is designed for potential use in various industries, including gaming, animation, and virtual environments.

Purpose:

Purpose:

The primary aim of this project is to provide a high-quality, realistic 3D asset that can be utilized by developers and artists in the gaming industry and other digital applications. By offering this model on 3D marketplaces, I aim to contribute valuable resources to other creators while also expanding my own portfolio and market presence.

Visual and Technical Details:

  • Modeling: The water gallon was modeled with precision to capture the exact proportions and characteristics of the actual AQUA gallon. Attention was paid to the unique structural features, including the ridged grip sections and the distinctive cap design.

  • Texturing: High-resolution textures were applied to replicate the translucent blue plastic material, which is typical of water gallons. The AQUA label was recreated with accurate colors, fonts, and placement to maintain the authenticity of the product.

  • Materials: The material setup includes shaders that mimic the semi-transparent nature of the plastic, as well as reflective properties to simulate the interaction of light with the gallon's surface.

  • Rendering: The render showcases the product in a clean, studio-like environment, highlighting the gallon's form and texture details. The image demonstrates how the model can be used in both close-up and distant shots, making it versatile for different applications.
