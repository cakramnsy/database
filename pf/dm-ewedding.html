Project Overview:

E-Wedding Invitation

E-Wedding Invitation

Background:

Background:

I was commissioned to design a digital wedding invitation for my client. The objective was to create a visually appealing, easy-to-share invitation that reflects the essence of the couple's special day. The final product was delivered as an E-Wedding Invitation, which could be shared digitally across various platforms.

Design Details:

Design Details:

The design is an elegant fusion of classic and modern aesthetics. Drawing from luxurious and intricate patterns, the background features a black, textured motif that exudes sophistication and grandeur. Gold accents highlight the details, giving the design a refined and regal touch.

The typography is a traditional serif font, adding a formal and timeless quality to the invitation. The date and time are displayed in a balanced and symmetrical format, with subtle dividers that reinforce the invitation's ornate style.

The couple's portrait is framed in a decorative gold border, serving as the focal point of the design. Their attire—bright red with intricate batik patterns—adds a cultural and personalized touch, emphasizing the celebration of their union.

Features:

  • Responsive Design: The invitation was designed to be accessible and fully responsive across all devices, particularly mobile phones.

  • Muted Sound for User Experience: To enhance the viewing experience, the invitation includes sound controls, ensuring that the audio doesn't automatically play unless chosen by the user.

  • Personalized Details: The client’s wedding date and personal story are central to the design, ensuring that the invitation reflects their unique journey.
