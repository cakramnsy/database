Design Concept:

Design Concept:

Shape Logo: The logo will be a minimalist, shape-based design related to sewing.

  • Color Identity: Dark green will be the sole color used, representing growth, stability, and quality associated with the garment industry.

  • Elements:

    • A white thread prominently featured, symbolizing the core function of sewing.

    • A needle with the white thread threaded through it, representing precision and craftsmanship.

    • A dark green circle background, providing a foundation and brand identity.
