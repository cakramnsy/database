Deliverables:

Deliverables:

Shopee Banner Ads (4 total): Standard Facebook banner size (assumed 1200px x 628px) featuring eye-catching visuals and clear messaging to promote Jahit Ind's Shopee store.

  • Design Style: Modern, creative, and attention-grabbing. Consider incorporating lifestyle imagery showcasing the apparel or using bold typography to highlight special offers.

  • TikTok Motion Graphic Video (1 video, 20 seconds, 9:16 aspect ratio): Short, engaging video ad specifically designed for TikTok to promote Jahit Ind's products and potentially their custom-made service.

    • Design Style: Short, dynamic, and trendy. Utilize music, text overlays, and creative transitions to showcase product variety and potentially customer testimonials (if provided).
