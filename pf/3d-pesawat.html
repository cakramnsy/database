Project Description:

Project Description:

This 3D animation was created as a personal project during the 2024 Indonesian election campaign for Prabowo Subianto and Gibran Rakabuming Raka. The primary goal of this short animation was to generate engagement on social media, utilizing the power of visual storytelling to captivate viewers during a significant political event.

Storyboard Overview:

Storyboard Overview:

The animation begins with an aerial view of the President of Indonesia's plane, reimagined with the branding and insignia of the Prabowo-Gibran campaign, symbolizing their potential future leadership. The plane is escorted by two Sukhoi fighter jets, flying in formation behind it, reinforcing the theme of strength and protection.

Creative Elements:

Creative Elements:

Airplane and Fighter Jets: The 3D models for the airplane and fighter jets were sourced from online repositories, allowing for efficient development while maintaining a high level of visual quality.

  • Animation: The movement of the plane and jets is smooth and realistic, with careful attention to the dynamics of flight, creating a visually compelling scene that aligns with the campaign's narrative.

  • Visual Style: The animation's style is sleek and modern, using dynamic camera angles and lighting to enhance the overall impact and convey a sense of authority and confidence.
