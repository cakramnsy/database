Project Goal:

Design modern and impactful graphic materials (brochure, calendar, wall clock) with mockups to support the PSI legislative campaign of Alana Maria, a young and energetic 24-year-old candidate.

Target Audience:

Target Audience:

This campaign aims to resonate with voters who value fresh perspectives and a youthful approach to politics. The design elements will target individuals who connect with a modern and progressive political leader.

Color Palette:

Color Palette:

Primary: The core color scheme will leverage PSI's red, maintaining brand identity.

  • Secondary: To enhance readability and create a modern aesthetic, consider incorporating a slightly lighter red or a contrasting color like white for specific elements.

Design Style:

Design Style:

Modern: Clean lines, minimalistic elements, and a focus on clear hierarchy will create a visually appealing and easy-to-understand design.

  • Energetic: Utilize dynamic compositions, impactful imagery, and possibly subtle pops of color to convey Alana's youthful energy and enthusiasm.

  • Professional: Maintain a level of sophistication to ensure the designs are perceived as credible and trustworthy.

Imagery:

Imagery:

High-quality photos provided by Alana Maria will be the foundation of the visual storytelling.

Copywriting:

Copywriting:

Clear, concise, and engaging text will highlight Alana's qualifications, policy positions, and vision for the future.

  • Use strong verbs and positive language to make the content impactful.

  • Tailor the copywriting to each design element, emphasizing relevant information for brochures, calendars, and wall clocks.
