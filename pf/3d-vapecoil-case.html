3D Product Design for "Baby Alien" Vape Coil Case - Biar Berkah

3D Product Design for "Baby Alien" Vape Coil Case - Biar Berkah

Background:

Background:

In 2022, I collaborated with a friend who owns the vape coil case brand Biar Berkah to create a 3D product design for their product, Baby Alien. The goal was to create an attractive and functional design for a vape coil case that would appeal to their target market. The case design had to reflect the brand's unique aesthetic while being practical for everyday use.

In 2022, I collaborated with a friend who owns the vape coil case brand Biar Berkah to create a 3D product design for their product, Baby Alien. The goal was to create an attractive and functional design for a vape coil case that would appeal to their target market. The case design had to reflect the brand's unique aesthetic while being practical for everyday use.

Objective:

Objective:

The primary objective was to design a visually striking and realistic 3D model of the Baby Alien vape coil case. The model would serve as a promotional image for the brand’s product listings and online store, representing the physical characteristics of the product with high fidelity.

Design Process:

  • Used Blender3D to model the vape coil case, focusing on accuracy in size, shape, and proportions based on the dimensions typically used for vape coils sold online.

  • Created a product that captures the unique branding of Biar Berkah, including the design of the logo and packaging for the Baby Alien product.

  • Rendered the final design with 1080x1080 HD output, ensuring that the quality was sufficient for both web and print use.

  • Visual and Technical Details:

    • Case Design: The case has a smooth, round exterior with a matte black finish on the lid and a transparent body to display the vape coil inside. The top features a vivid label with the Biar Berkah logo and the "Baby Alien" branding, using bright red and turquoise colors to make the design pop.

    • Vape Coil: The inner coil design is detailed with a metallic texture, precisely modeled to reflect the technical aesthetics of vape coils.

    • Wireframe Visualization: Created wireframe renderings to demonstrate the precision of the 3D modeling, emphasizing the complexity and structure of the coil design.

  • Rendering:

    • Rendered in Blender3D with high-quality textures and lighting to create realistic reflections and shadows, ensuring the product’s details were accurately depicted.

    • Delivered both rendered images and wireframe views to showcase the product from different angles, enhancing the technical presentation of the design.

  • Hypothetical Outcomes:

    • Helped establish a distinctive product image for Biar Berkah’s "Baby Alien" line, enhancing its online presence with high-quality visuals.

    • Contributed to the brand’s ability to market their products more effectively with professional 3D renders that align with industry standards for product promotion.
