Deliverables:

Deliverables:

Instagram Story Open Invitation (1 image): A visually engaging Instagram story graphic promoting the KLAMBY graphic designer recruitment test.

  • Size: Optimized for Instagram story dimensions (assumed 1080px x 1920px).

  • Design Style: Utilize an imperial theme with creative elements to showcase KLAMBY's brand identity.

  • Color Palette: Feature a dominant blue background to grab attention and potentially incorporate gold or other regal colors to complement the imperial theme.

  • Model: Include a photo of a female model (if approved by KLAMBY) to add visual interest and potentially connect with a broader audience.

  • Information: Clearly communicate essential details like the job title, event date and time (Saturday, August 6, 2023, 3:30 PM), and location (Senayan City, Jakarta, Indonesia).

  • Call to Action: Include a clear call to action, such as "Swipe Up to Apply" or "DM for Details."
