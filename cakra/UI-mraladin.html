Project Overview:

Mister Aladin App

Mister Aladin App

Project Goal:

Project Goal:

This project aimed to redesign a new life into the Mister Aladin mobile application, a leading online hotel booking platform in Indonesia. The focus in 2022 was on enhancing user experience (UX) to streamline the booking process, personalize the user journey, and ultimately increase conversions.

Challenges:

Challenges:

Cutthroat Competition: The Indonesian travel booking market is a battleground. User expectations for a seamless and efficient booking experience are constantly on the rise. Mister Aladin needed to stay ahead of the curve to retain its user base.

  • User Pain Points: Understanding user frustrations was key. This could have involved user research through surveys, focus groups, or app analytics to identify pain points. Common issues might have been:

    • Difficulty finding the perfect hotel due to a cluttered interface or lack of intuitive filtering options.

    • A cumbersome booking process with too many steps or excessive form fields.

    • Absence of personalized recommendations leading to feelings of information overload and decision fatigue.

Solutions:

Solutions:

UI Refresh: A modern and intuitive UI refresh could have involved:

  • Information Hierarchy: Reorganizing information for better readability, prioritizing key details about hotels at a glance.

  • White Space: Utilizing white space effectively to avoid overwhelming users and make the app feel more user-friendly.

  • Iconography and Visuals: Leveraging clear and consistent icons and high-quality visuals to enhance usability and make the app more visually appealing.

  • Streamlined Booking Flow: Simplifying the booking process could have included:

    • Reduced Steps: Minimizing the number of clicks required to complete a booking, optimizing the user flow.

    • Smart Forms: Implementing features like auto-fill or pre-populated forms based on user profiles to reduce data entry.

    • Clear Progress Indicators: Providing users with visual cues to show them where they are in the booking process and how much further they have to go.

  • Personalized Recommendations: By leveraging user data and search history, Mister Aladin could suggest relevant hotels based on:

    • Preferences: Utilizing user-specified preferences like travel style (luxury, budget, family-friendly) to filter and recommend suitable hotels.

    • Budget: Factoring in budget constraints to surface hotels that meet the user's financial comfort zone.

    • Past Behavior: Using past search history and bookings to suggest similar hotels or locations the user has shown interest in.

  • Localization Focus: Highlighting hotels that cater to specific Indonesian travel needs:

    • Unique Experiences: Promoting hotels that offer traditional experiences or local attractions.

    • Traveler Types: Curating hotel selections for specific traveler groups, such as families with young children or business professionals requiring amenities like conference rooms.

App Functionality:

App Functionality:

Doctor Management:

  • View real-time doctor status (present, in surgery, on break).

  • Update doctor status throughout the day. (Optional: Allow doctors to update their own status)

  • Manage doctor schedules and availability.

  • Queue Management:

    • Create and manage queues for upcoming patients based on appointments and walk-ins.

    • Track patient arrival and integrate with appointment scheduling systems (if applicable).

    • Effectively handle missed queues and reschedule appointments if needed.

    • Display estimated wait times for patients based on doctor schedules and queue positions.

  • Checkout Process:

    • Streamline patient checkout process after appointments.

    • Integrate with billing systems (optional).

    • Allow for appointment follow-up options (surveys, feedback forms).

  • Reporting & Analytics: (Optional, but valuable)

    • Generate reports on doctor activity, patient wait times, queue lengths, and missed appointments.

    • Offer data visualization for trends and insights to improve clinic efficiency.
