Problem Statement:

Problem Statement:

BreadTalk, a leading bread factory in Indonesia, sought to enhance its customer experience by revamping its mobile app. The existing app lacked user-friendly navigation, and customers faced challenges in discovering new products, placing orders seamlessly, and staying engaged with the brand.

Research Phase:

Research Phase:

User Research

Design Phase:

Design Phase:

Information Architecture

  • Reorganized the app's information architecture to promote easier navigation, placing frequently used features at the forefront.

Wireframing

  • Developed wireframes to illustrate the revised app structure, including clear product categories and a streamlined checkout process.

Prototyping

  • Crafted interactive prototypes to simulate the user journey, incorporating features like a simplified ordering process, product recommendations, and a loyalty program.

Visual Design

  • Refreshed the app's visual identity with a clean and modern design, integrating BreadTalk's branding elements. Applied a consistent visual style throughout the app.
