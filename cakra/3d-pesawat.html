Project Goal:

Project Goal:

This project aims to create a stunning 3D aircraft animation celebrating Prabowo & Gibran's inauguration as President and Vice President.

Concept:

Concept:

The animation will showcase the main presidential aircraft (Air Force One) flying smoothly above a layer of volumetric clouds in a bright daylight setting.

  • Two Sukhoi fighter jets will escort the Air Force One throughout the animation, symbolizing strength and security.

  • The animation will be set to the music "Hans Zimmer - Flight" for a dramatic and uplifting effect.

Storyboard:

Storyboard:

Intro: The animation begins with a title card displaying the text "PRABOWO GIBRAN" in an animated fashion.

  • Main Animation:

    • The camera pans up to reveal Air Force One flying majestically above a layer of volumetric clouds.

    • Two Sukhoi fighter jets fly alongside the Air Force One, maintaining close formation.

    • The camera follows the aircraft as they continue their journey towards their destination.

  • Outro: The animation fades out as the aircraft continue their flight.
