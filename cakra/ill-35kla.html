Project Goal:

Project Goal:

This project focuses on creating a logo for the upcoming 35 Years Kla Project Concert, a significant event for the renowned Indonesian band "Kla Project."

Key Design Elements:

Key Design Elements:

Text: The primary focus will be the number "35," representing the band's anniversary celebration.

  • Numerical Design: The "35" will be designed creatively, potentially incorporating elements that symbolize the band's musical style or journey.

  • Color Palette: A vibrant and eye-catching color scheme will be utilized, including orange, light blue, yellow, and red.

Logo Applications:

Logo Applications:

Videotron Display: The logo will be designed for prominent display on video screens at Gelora Bung Karno Stadium a month before the concert (October 31st, 2023).

  • Gate Entrance Logo: The logo will be used for branding the concert gates, ensuring easy identification and a cohesive visual experience for attendees.

  • Social Media Promotion: The logo will be adapted for social media platforms to generate excitement and anticipation for the concert.
