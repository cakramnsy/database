Problem Statements:

Problem Statements:

Lack of Coverage Information: The current website lacks information about HIGO's Wi-Fi coverage areas across Indonesia.

  • Unclear Platform Explanation: There's no clear explanation regarding HIGO's Wi-Fi advertising platform and its functionalities.

  • Outdated Partner List: The list of HIGO partners by region is not up-to-date, making it difficult for users to find relevant advertising opportunities.

  • Missing Internet Speed Details: Information on internet speed specifications provided by HIGO for users is missing.

  • Absent Location Feature: The website lacks a map feature to display the nearest Wi-Fi zones and their distance from the user's location.

Proposed UI Design Solutions:

Proposed UI Design Solutions:

Web App:

  • Homepage:

    • Hero banner showcasing the benefits of HIGO's Wi-Fi advertising platform with a clear call to action (CTA) button.

    • Interactive map of Indonesia highlighting areas with HIGO Wi-Fi coverage. Clicking on a specific region will display a list of partners and zone details.

    • Simple explanation of the Wi-Fi advertising platform and its target audience.

  • Partners Page:

    • Searchable and filterable directory of HIGO partners categorized by region and industry.

    • Partner profiles with contact information, website links, and Wi-Fi zone details.

  • Coverage Page:

    • Detailed map showcasing all Wi-Fi zone locations across Indonesia.

    • Ability to filter zones based on internet speed specifications offered by HIGO.

  • Resources Page:

    • FAQs section addressing common user questions about the platform, pricing, and coverage.

    • Case studies showcasing successful Wi-Fi advertising campaigns on HIGO's platform.

Mobile App:

  • Home Screen:

    • Simplified version of the web app's homepage with key functionalities.

    • Access to user account and campaign management tools (if applicable).

  • Location Services:

    • Map displaying nearby Wi-Fi zones with distance information.

    • Ability to search for specific zones by location or partner name.

  • Partner Directory:

    • List of HIGO partners in the vicinity with basic contact details.

Additional Considerations:

Additional Considerations:

Responsive Design: Ensure the UI adapts seamlessly across different screen sizes (desktop, tablet, mobile).

  • User-Friendly Interface: Implement intuitive navigation, clear calls to action, and visually appealing design elements.

  • Data Security: Prioritize data security measures to protect user and partner information.

  • Multilingual Support: Consider offering the platform in Bahasa Indonesia and English to cater to a wider audience.
