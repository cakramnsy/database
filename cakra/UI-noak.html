App Functionality:

App Functionality:

Doctor Management:

  • View real-time doctor status (present, in surgery, on break).

  • Update doctor status throughout the day. (Optional: Allow doctors to update their own status)

  • Manage doctor schedules and availability.

  • Queue Management:

    • Create and manage queues for upcoming patients based on appointments and walk-ins.

    • Track patient arrival and integrate with appointment scheduling systems (if applicable).

    • Effectively handle missed queues and reschedule appointments if needed.

    • Display estimated wait times for patients based on doctor schedules and queue positions.

  • Checkout Process:

    • Streamline patient checkout process after appointments.

    • Integrate with billing systems (optional).

    • Allow for appointment follow-up options (surveys, feedback forms).

  • Reporting & Analytics: (Optional, but valuable)

    • Generate reports on doctor activity, patient wait times, queue lengths, and missed appointments.

    • Offer data visualization for trends and insights to improve clinic efficiency.
