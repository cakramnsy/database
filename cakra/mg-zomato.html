Animation Concept:

Animation Concept:

Storyboard: A 20-second animation featuring two scenes:

  • Scene 1 (Car): A Honda WRV 2023 gracefully navigates a winding road with uneven terrain, highlighting the car's smooth handling and stability thanks to Showa's suspension.

  • Scene 2 (Motorcycle): A Kawasaki ZX 25 R 2023 tackles a bumpy racetrack with exceptional control, demonstrating the motorcycle's superior shock absorption with Showa's suspension.

  • Camera Movement: Utilize dynamic camera angles and movements to create an engaging and visually impactful experience.

  • Lighting: Maximize lighting effects to achieve an epic and elegant aesthetic, adhering to corporate style standards.
