BIAR BERKAH Coil Case (3D Product Design)

Project Goal:

Project Goal:

Design a functional and visually appealing coil case for BIAR BERKAH's built-in coil parts for Mod Vapes.

Design a functional and visually appealing coil case for BIAR BERKAH's built-in coil parts for Mod Vapes.

Role:

Role:

Tools:

Start:

Start:

Product Specifications:

Product Specifications:

Shape: Round with a twist-to-lock lid for secure closure.

  • Lid Diameter: 3 cm

  • Main Case Diameter: 2.5 cm (to accommodate 2 coils)

  • Lid Color: Black Metallic

  • Main Case: Transparent - Depth: 1 mm (interior) & Height: 4 cm

  • Logo: BIAR BERKAH logo positioned in the center of the lid.

My Role:

  • Created a transparent main case with the specified depth and height.
